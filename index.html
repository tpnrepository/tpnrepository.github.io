About Test Professionals Network, Wellington

Test Professionals Network, Wellington is the leading forum for promoting excellence in systems and software testing. We promote the importance of software testing by holding regular meetings with presentations by local and international speakers to develop awareness of good industry practices in software testing. We help create networking opportunities for members to share knowledge, experience and ideas and represent the interests of the group’s members with other forums.
